Nidaan Commercial Building

Nidaan is a Residential cum Commercial Building completed in the year 2017. It is on road and is located in Karamtoli Road, Near SN Yadav Alley (Beside Celebrations), Ranchi, Jharkhand. The Building is 'B+G+6' (Basement + Ground Floor + 6 Floors). Ground, First and Second Floors are Commercial spaces amounting to a total area of about 19500 sqft.

Nidaan Residential Flats

Third to Sixth Floor of Nidaan contains 12 Residential Flats amounting to a total area of 20000 sqft. The building is in the close to the heart of the city yet surrounded by greenery on three sides and by main road on the front. There is basement parking as well as outside parking for residents, visiting guests and customers.

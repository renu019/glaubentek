About Us

Glaubentek Buildeco Private Limited specialises in the field of Real Estate and Infrastructure Development. We partake in both Public and Private Contracts. Our expertise ranges from construction of Multi-storied Apartments, Residential Buildings like Bungalows, Villas, Duplex and Luxury Houses to Construction of Roads, Shopping Complexes, Industrial Buildings, Godown Sheds, Commercial Showrooms & Buildings. In addition, we are happy and much obliged to serve our clients only in the capacity of Consultants if need be.


The company itself was incorporated on 3rd October but it's foundation stone was laid years ago. Despite it being new to the industry, The Team had already completed a Substantial project named NIDAAN ‐ A Residential and Commercial complex in the heart of Ranchi.


Our team is headed by Highly Qualified and Experienced Professionals with Post-Graduations from reputed Universities around the World. Moreover, The Technical team being the backbone of the company hail from reputed Central Government Organisations racking combined experience of more than 60 years in the field of Infrastructure and Real Estate Development, handling several multi-crore projects over the given time.


We pride ourselves on our Values and Work Ethic. Our most cherished values are embedded in our name itself wherein 'Glauben' means Trust & 'Tek' means Technology. Our very essence is defined by the trust between Client and Us under which we strive to be your Trusted Technical partner with the capability to handle your dreams with Technical precision and deliver Time-bound result. Moreover, Build-Eco is a promise towards dynamic learning and practice of Environment friendly techniques in all the projects we undertake to the best of the limitations.


There is also a sense of Discipline, Organisation and Efficiency among the Management, Employees and Suppliers which is a harbinger of genuine respect between everyone, translating to similar relationship with clients. We are determined to serve our clients with utmost integrity.
